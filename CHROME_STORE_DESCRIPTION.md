## FAQs (for store description or support)

**Q: Where are my notes stored?**
A: All notes are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API. They never leave your computer.

**Q: Can I sync notes across devices?**
A: No, by design. NewTab Notes is privacy-focused and keeps all data local. This ensures your notes are 100% private.

**Q: Will this work on mobile?**
A: NewTab Notes is designed for desktop Chrome browsers. Mobile browsers don't support new tab overrides.

**Q: Can I export my notes?**
A: Currently, there's no export UI. All notes are in Chrome's local storage. Advanced users can access them via browser dev tools.

**Q: Does this work offline?**
A: Yes! NewTab Notes works completely offline since everything is local.

**Q: How do I back up my notes?**
A: Your notes are stored in Chrome's profile. Back up your Chrome profile to preserve notes.

**Q: Why no dark mode?**
A: We focused on a clean, Notion-inspired white theme for simplicity. Dark mode may come in future updates if requested.

**Q: Can I organize notes into folders?**
A: No, NewTab Notes uses a flat structure with tabs. This keeps the interface simple and fast.

**Q: Is this open source?**
A: The code is transparent and auditable. We may open-source it in the future.

**Q: How do I uninstall?**
A: Right-click the extension icon â†’ "Remove from Chrome" or go to chrome://extensions and click "Remove".
